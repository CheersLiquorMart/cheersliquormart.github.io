Employee Benefits Overview

Information about benefits available to Cheers Liquor Mart employees.

At Cheers Liquor Mart, we are committed to providing competitive benefits to support our employees. Below is an overview of our current and upcoming benefits:

Retirement Plan – 401(k) Coming Soon

We are in the process of establishing a 401(k) plan, which will replace the Simple IRA previously offered by the former ownership. This new plan will be accessible through Paychex Flex, allowing you to:

  • View balances
  • Manage investments
  • Adjust withholdings/contributions

If you participated in the previous Simple IRA, you'll need to check with (insert contact name here) regarding its status and your options. You may choose to keep it as is, roll it into the new 401(k), or explore other alternatives. Note that Cheers Liquor Mart is not affiliated with the prior retirement plan, as it was part of the former company.

Workers' Compensation

We provide workers' compensation coverage through Pinnacol Assurance. If you experience a work-related injury, you must use a preferred provider for treatment.

View the list of preferred providers and procedures

Paid Time Off (PTO)

Eligible employees receive vacation and sick leave in accordance with state regulations. Details on accruals and usage can be found in the employee handbook.

View PTO policies in the employee handbook
