Delivery Driver Policy

Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes guidelines for Cheers Liquor Mart delivery drivers to ensure safe, efficient, and legally compliant delivery operations. All delivery personnel must adhere to these guidelines.

Vehicle Safety Requirements

  1. Daily Vehicle Inspection: Drivers must perform a vehicle inspection before each shift including:
    • Checking tire pressure and condition
    • Testing all lights (headlights, brake lights, turn signals)
    • Checking fluid levels (oil, coolant, windshield washer)
    • Ensuring brakes are functioning properly
    • Confirming seatbelts work correctly
  2. Reporting Maintenance Issues: Any vehicle issues must be reported immediately using the Vehicle Maintenance Report form.
  3. Emergency Equipment: All delivery vehicles must be equipped with:
    • First aid kit
    • Fire extinguisher
    • Reflective triangles or flares
    • Flashlight
    • Basic tool kit

Delivery Procedures

  1. ID Verification: Drivers must verify the customer is 21+ years old by checking a valid government-issued photo ID for every delivery.
  2. Signature Collection: A signature must be obtained for all deliveries using the electronic delivery system.
  3. Payment Handling: Drivers must follow cash handling procedures and reconcile all transactions at the end of each shift.
  4. Undeliverable Orders: If a delivery cannot be completed (customer not home, failed ID verification), the driver must:
    • Contact the store for instructions
    • Document the reason for non-delivery
    • Return undelivered items to the store

Safety Guidelines

  1. Proper Lifting Techniques:
    • Bend at the knees, not the waist
    • Keep the load close to your body
    • Do not twist while lifting
    • Use dollies or hand trucks for heavy loads
  2. Weather Safety:
    • Adjust driving to weather conditions
    • During severe weather, contact management for delivery adjustments
  3. Personal Safety:
    • Maintain awareness of surroundings at all times
    • Do not enter residences
    • Keep minimal cash on hand
    • Park in well-lit areas when possible

Accident and Emergency Procedures

  1. Vehicle Accidents:
    • Ensure safety and call 911 if needed
    • Exchange information with other parties
    • Take photos of damage and the scene
    • Contact store management immediately
    • Complete an accident report within 24 hours
  2. Medical Emergencies:
    • Call 911 for serious injuries
    • Use first aid kit for minor injuries
    • Report all injuries to management
