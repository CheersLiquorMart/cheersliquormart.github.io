Important information about medical providers and procedures for workplace injuries.

What to Do If You Are Hurt on the Job

  1. Tell Rob or Kyle as soon as possible.
    • If you can't reach them, inform a manager or Christina immediately.
  2. Get medical care.
    • Use one of the approved providers listed above for treatment.
  3. Complete an injury report.
    • Your manager or Christina will help you with the paperwork.
  4. Follow the doctor's orders.
    • Stick to the treatment plan so you can recover quickly.
  5. Keep your employer updated.
    • Let Rob, Kyle, or your manager know about your progress and any work restrictions.

If you have questions, ask the individuals listed above or contact Pinnacol Assurance at 800-873-7242.
