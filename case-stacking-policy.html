Case Stacking Guidelines

Purpose:

Well-stacked displays attract customers, increase sales, and keep the store looking professional. Proper stacking ensures safety, prevents waste, and helps maintain a great shopping experience.

Acceptable Case Stacks:

  • Stacks of 5, 4, 3, or 2 cases per row.
  • Stacks of 4, 3, or 2 cases per row.

Unacceptable Case Stacks:

  • Stacks with 4 or 2 cases in a row (unless specified otherwise).
  • Stacks arranged as 3/2.

    (Some minor exceptions may apply.)

Important Stacking Rules:

  • Do not shrink stacks by disposing of empty cases.
  • Keep empty boxes and turn them upside down to maintain stack height.
  • A two-case stack is not an appropriate display.
  • Cases should be stacked properly to enhance visibility rather than for storage.

REMEMBER:

A well-stocked, attractive display keeps customers coming back and drives sales, which keeps the store running smoothly and benefits the entire team.

STACK IT HIGH AND WATCH IT FLY!
